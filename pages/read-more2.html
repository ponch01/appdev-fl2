Virtual Try-Ons, Interactive Displays, and Immersive Experiences Virtual try-ons allow customers to see how clothes or accessories would look on them before they buy, reducing the need for fitting rooms and leading to more confident purchases. Interactive product displays bring products to life, letting customers explore features and benefits in a dynamic way. Imagine pointing your phone at a piece of furniture and seeing it virtually placed in your living room to assess size and style. Immersive in-store experiences, like virtual showrooms, can showcase a wider range of products or even let you see how furniture might look in your own home, all without leaving the store.
